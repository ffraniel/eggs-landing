Ova The Hills

Situated on the river Wharfe and in the foot of the Yorkshire Dales Addingham is surrounded by stunning scenery. The village nestles on the side of the wharfe valley between Ilkley and Bolton Abbey. The surrounding hills and moorsides provide some considerable beauty and make it popular destination with visitors.
